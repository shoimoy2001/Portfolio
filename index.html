About Me

I am a cybersecurity undergraduate at Touro University with a strong interest in secure software engineering and defensive system design. I have hands-on experience working as an IT Support Technician, where I supported users, troubleshot operating systems, managed accounts, and resolved endpoint and system-level issues in real-world environments.

I enjoy understanding how applications and systems are attacked and applying that knowledge to build secure, reliable, and maintainable software. My interests include application security, security tool development, and designing systems that balance usability with strong security controls.

I am actively developing my skills at the intersection of software engineering and cybersecurity through academic coursework, personal projects, and continuous hands-on learning.
